Minecraft Chest Sound Effect

Scan this QR Code

URL: https://jhacksman.github.io/mc-chest-soundfx-app/

How to Use:

  1. Scan the QR code with your phone's camera
  2. Open the link in Chrome browser
  3. Grant camera permissions when prompted
  4. Click "Start Camera" button
  5. Place your phone inside a chest with the back camera facing up
  6. When you open the chest, it will play the Minecraft chest opening sound
  7. When you close the chest, it will play the Minecraft chest closing sound
